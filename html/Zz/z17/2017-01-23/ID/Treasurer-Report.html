I am prepared to create event tickets with ActBlue for the upcoming SCC meeting that the Interior Democrats will host. I am expecting a price and date with possibly early bird and or young democrat discounts. Have we determined date and price yet?

I contacted Denali State Bank and inquired about the $5.00 service charge we are once again incurring. Denali State Bank does not offer a no fees account for businesses or non profit organizations. Their best is the account we have which incurs no fees only if a minimum balance of $2,000.00 is maintained. I then called Alaska USA FCU who waives the Minimum Daily Account requirement on a checking account for a non profit. So we could save up to $60.00 a year in bank fees when we cannot maintain a $2,000.00 minimum balance by switching to Alaska USA but it will take quite a bit of effort to switch all to a new bank account. We do not incur any new fees by opening a new checking account with Alaska USA FCU as long as we qualify as a Non Profit organization to start the process of switching to a new bank account.
